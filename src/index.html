About Me

I am a huge nerd for Mother Nature and am in love with discovering Her mysteries as I hike, bike, ski, swim, and whatever else through Her lands. My second love is problem solving, and what better tool to solve problems with than software? I am entirely self taught (though I contend that Philosophy and CS are almost identical) and have worked professionally as a Software Engineer since 2022. I am trained in Python, Ruby (and Rails), Javascript, and C#. I have worked on machine learning models, written data science software, build autonomous robots for private companies, and have worked as a full stack web developer. Am I a Jack of All Trades? NO! Do you know how many trades there are (that would be crazy)!? What I do bring is an ability and love for learning. Give me a problem and it will (eventually) be solved.
